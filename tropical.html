Tropical
Feast

Ingredients

  • 4 square slices of watermelon
  • 4 slices of star fruit
  • 1 passion fruit, cut in half
  • 1 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup of almond milk

    • Instruction:Arrange all the cut fruit on a platter. Take out the 1 cup of greek yogurt and frozen blueberries into the blender. Then add the 3/4 cup of almong milk into the mixure. Blend until thick consistency. Scoop the mixure into a bowl and put it at the center of the platter. Finally, add your favorite toppings and enjoy!
