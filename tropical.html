Tropical Feast

Ingredients

  • 4 square slices of watermelon
  • 4 slices of star fruit
  • 1 passion fruit, cut in half
  • 1 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of frozen blueberries

    • Instruction:Arrange all the fruit on a platter. Scoop into a bowl greek yogurt. Next, blend the frozen blueberries. Finally, add favorite toppings and enjoy!
