Blueberry Banana

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 of a frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup of granola

    • Instruction:Add all ingredients and blend, and start adding the 3/4 cup of milk. Mix until smooth and creamy. Next, scoop into a bowl, and add desired toppings and enjoy!
