Blueberry
Banana

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 of a frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup of granola

    • Instruction:Add the forzen blueberries and banana in blender. Next, start adding slowy the 3/4 cup of milk. Mix until smooth and creamy. Finally, scoop into a bowl the mixure, and add desired toppings such as granola, blueberries, and banana slices, and enjoy!
